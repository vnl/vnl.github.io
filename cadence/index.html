what it does
Calendar as home
Your training lives on the calendar. Every session, rest day, and planned workout visible at a glance.
Log in under 60 seconds
Title, notes, tags — done. Free-text notes are a first-class feature, not a fallback.
Plan ahead
Create sessions on any future date. Planned entries are visually distinct from completed ones.
Two-a-days supported
Multiple entries per day, stacked on the calendar cell. No artificial limits.
Tags for filtering
Tag entries with your own labels — legs, upper, cardio — and filter by tag to review consistency.
Rest & recovery days
Mark rest and recovery as distinct entry types. Your full week is always visible, not just the sessions.
Duplicate any entry
Found a session to repeat? Duplicate it to a new date in two taps. Title, notes, and tags carry across.
Private by default
Training data is stored on your device. No ads. No subscriptions. One purchase, yours to keep.
